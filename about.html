About Us

Vision

Solar Taxi is an enterprise with the vision to be the largest EV company in Africa.

Mission

We seek to accelerate Ghana towards green and affordable transportation services by leveraging the energy from the sun and innovations from the shared economy. We hope to reduce carbon emissions and the cost of delivery and transport services to individual and businesses across the country through our values (Gender Empowerment, Respect & Teamwork, Service Excellence, Innovation).
